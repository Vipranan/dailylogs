jupiterHR is a Canadian career coaching and recruitment platform that connects Black professionals, especially in the technology sector, with equitable job opportunities. Founded in 2015 by Jermaine Murray, the company empowers Black talent through personalized services such as career coaching, resume writing, and interview preparation, emphasizing storytelling to help candidates effectively communicate their career narratives.
